How to Use SMS & Drip

1. Getting Started

Start by locating the app in your Monday.com workspace.

  1. Navigate to "Workspace apps" in the sidebar.
  2. Find SMS & Drip and open it.
  3. Click "Authorize" to grant permissions.

2. Features

A. Send Campaign (Sequence Starter)

Part 1: Create Campaign (Dashboard)

First, build your drip sequence visually.

  • Open the SMS & Drip app dashboard.
  • Click "New Campaign" and give it a name.
  • Add your SMS blocks and delays.

Part 2: Configure Automation (Workflow)

Next, trigger this campaign from your board.

  • Go to Mondy.com Workflows (Automations).
  • Select your trigger (e.g., "Status changes to...").
  • Choose action: "SMS & Drip: Sequence Starter".
  • Map required fields:
    • • Columns: Map Board ID, Item ID, and Phone Column.
    • • Settings: Select Twilio Account and your created Campaign.

B. Stop Campaign (Smart Stopper)

Cancel active campaigns when an item's status changes.

  • In Workflows, select your stop trigger (e.g., "Status changes to Done").
  • Choose action: "SMS & Drip: Smart Stopper".
  • This immediately stops all scheduled messages for that item.

C. Send One Message

Send an ad-hoc message via automation.

  • In Workflows, select your trigger.
  • Choose action: "SMS & Drip: Send Message".
  • Configure Twilio Account and type your message directly in the action.

3. Replies & Webhooks

Important: We can only enforce compliance (STOP/UNSUBSCRIBE) if we receive the replies. This requires a properly configured webhook.

Our system attempts to auto-configure your Twilio number. If you have an existing webhook, we will NOT override it.

Manual Configuration:

If replies are not working, manually set the "Messaging Webhook" URL in your Twilio Console to:

https://live1-service-33080281-d3a8e938.us.monday.app/api/twilio/reply

4. Compliance

Once webhooks are active, we handle compliance automatically.

Opt-Out Keywords:

  • If a user replies with STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, or CANCEL:
  • ✓ Future messages are blocked immediately.
  • ✓ Item status on your board updates to "Unsubscribed".
