How to Use SMS & Drip

1. Getting Started

Welcome to SMS & Drip! To start sending messages directly from Monday.com, you first need to add the Board View to your board.

  1. Open your board in Monday.com.
  2. Click the + icon next to your views tab.
  3. Search for "SMS & Drip" and click Add View.
Screenshot: Adding the Board View

2. Connecting Twilio

You'll need your Twilio Account SID and Auth Token to connect your account.

  • Log in to your Twilio Console.
  • Copy the SID and Auth Token from the dashboard.
  • In the SMS & Drip app settings, paste these credentials and hit Save.
Screenshot: Twilio Credentials Input

3. Creating Your First Campaign

Use our visual builder to design your drip campaigns.

Step 1: Click "New Campaign" and give it a name.

Step 2: Add specific triggers (e.g., "Status changes to 'Won'").

Step 3: Drag and drop SMS blocks to create your sequence.

Screenshot: Visual Campaign Builder

4. Automation Features

Stop on Status Change

Automatically stop sending messages if the item's status changes on your board (e.g., from "Lead" to "Qualified").

Auto-stop on Reply

If a user replies to one of your messages, the sequence will automatically pause to allow for personal follow-up.

5. Compliance

We handle compliance automatically.

If a recipient replies STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, or CANCEL:

  • They are instantly removed from all future campaigns.
  • Their status on your board is updated to "Unsubscribed".
