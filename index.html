Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own Twilio account?

Yes! This integration uses a "Bring Your Own Twilio" (BYOT) model. You securely connect your own Twilio Account SID and Auth Token. This ensures you pay standard Twilio carrier rates directly with absolutely zero markup or hidden SMS fees from us.

What happens when someone replies?

If someone replies "STOP", "UNSUBSCRIBE", or "CANCEL", Twilio handles the carrier block, and our webhook gracefully halts any active drip campaigns for that lead automatically. You can also configure automated triggers when positive responses are received.

What types of phone numbers are supported?

You can use any active sender number provisioned in your Twilio account. This seamlessly includes 10DLC local numbers, verified Toll-Free numbers, and high-throughput Short Codes.
